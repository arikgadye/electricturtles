ELECTRIC TURTLES High Voltage Sex Rock

“Arik sailed across the atlantic ocean from California, to a new land filled with wonder and Hand Brotchen; Germany. It was here where he crossed paths with Christoph, a good German boy from Dresden. The two traveled to lands near and far, when they came across a great ocean, known as the “Baltic Sea”. It was here they caught and gutted their first fish together, drank their first mulled wine together, and learned that the 7 seas are both made of beauty, and peril. It was through this peril that something was born, something hidden deep inside their bones... By the holy god of rock and roll himself, Arik and Christoph created something magnificent, a sound for the generations past present and future: Sex Rock. Music that will make you take your pants off. Combining Christophs affinity for garage rock, and Ariks affinity for funk and hip-hop, ELECTRIC TURTLES is an explosive melting pot from 2 different sides of the world. If your head isn’t moving, if your feet aren’t dancing, if you legs aren’t shaking, well, you simply aren’t listening to ELECTRIC TURTLES. They take you on a ride of a lifetime, from the laid back / aggressive hip-hop & garage rock “The Takedown”, to the dark and mysterious “Wasteland.” Melodies, syncopations, lyrics and all, ELECTRIC TURTLES hits every corner of your soul.” - the old testament
